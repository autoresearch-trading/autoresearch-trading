**Definitions:**
- `fundingPnL d r_f T` — constant-rate funding P&L: `-d * r_f * T`
- `fundingPnLSum d r_f T` — variable-rate funding P&L as a sum over periods
- `fundingCostPerTrade E_rf T_bar H` — funding cost per trade: `|E_rf| * T_bar * H`
- `T_max alpha E_rf H` — max hold duration before funding exceeds alpha
- `expectedFundingCost q E_rf` — expected funding cost for a mixed long/short strategy

- `funding_pnl_sign_opposite'`: F > 0 when d·r_f < 0
- `fundingPnLSum_const`: Variable-rate formula reduces to constant-rate formula

**Claim 2 — Funding drag exceeds alpha (2 theorems):**
